Monstera is an excellent decoration to your room, if you have enough space for it. Growing in tropical jungles, it uses the tree trunks to lean on and climb closer to the sun. In your appartment, however, they have to rely on moss posts. In the wild, they can grow dozens feet tall, covering entire tree trunk with their huge segmented leaves. If you're especially lucky, you can find variegated Monstera. It's a relatively rare mutation, creating white patches on the leaves. They look stunning, although the white parts usually dont last long and dry out. It's not your fault though, it's how nature works! Monstera doesn't like direct sunlight and prefers to stay in full or half-shade, slowly climbing the vertical surfaces towards the light. It likes high humidity, so sprinkle some water on it's leaves and air roots regularly. Its leaves can grow as big as 2 ft in diameter, so make sure Monstera has enough space and dont cram it into the tight corner.
