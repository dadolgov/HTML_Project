Rubber Tree, also known as Ficus, has gain it's reputation as an "office plant". Indeed, it can grow big and strong without much mainteanance. Just water it and give it some sun, and be rewarded with a new leaf every few days! In the wild, rubber trees can grow dozens of feet in height - it was called a "tree" for a reason! It's roots are growing very fast, and the plant's size is limited only by the size of a pot. Well, and a size of your room, too.
