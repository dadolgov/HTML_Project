Originating from hot African and Middle Eastern regions, Spider Plants became welcome guests in millions of houses across the world. Their gorgeous greei and white leaves can fit into any interrior. Both the plant and roots are growing very fast. Once it filled its pot, it grows offshoots with tiny copies of itself, looking for a new patch of soil around it. Leave them be and enjoy a gorgeous cascading "beard" made of offshoots, hanging off the pot. If you want, cut the shoots off and put them into water or soil: in a couple of weeks they'll grow roots and become a standalone plant. Feel free to give it to friends and colleagues!
